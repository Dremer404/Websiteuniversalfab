Footage from Select

As they transform from a membership benefits card to the new Amex Centurion Black card, Select approached ONTO to figure out how to make their story tangible with a complete redesign that communicates their unique, app-centric luxury card. We needed to redesign digital assets as well as the card itself, providing top-tier engineering to realize the creative vision as well.

Footage from Select

DESIGN NOTES

One of the key differences between Select and other luxury cards is how app-native its functionality is. We made this difference a choice the user can interact with via a beautiful, animated 3D interface on landing.

The web experience of traditional black cards often looks more like spec sheets in direct mail, with detailed rate charts. We brought an immersive 3D approach with multiple opportunities for user interaction.

PRODUCTION

Because of the step-jump in design quality for this project, it was especially important to bring a full team of award-winning design engineers that were ready-to-go. Production also involved designing the physical new Select card and tastefully integrating requirements, including the security chip and Mastercard logo.
