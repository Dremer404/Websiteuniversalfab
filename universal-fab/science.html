Science

Footage from Science

Science applies AI to healthcare administration and medical research. Our challenge was to make technical concepts accessible to a broad audience that included healthcare executives. For this, we received an Awwwards Site of the Month designation, a CSS Design Award, an FWA Award, and had an in-depth site review by an Awwwards panel.

Footage from Science

DESIGN NOTES

We developed a novel aesthetic that invoked organic settings to emphasize medical discovery that Science powers across clinical and research settings. These objects are alien, and create an implicit need to understand.

The visual content emphasizes the intelligence of the system via lighting and motion. Intelligent objects interacting with inert ones cause the latter to light up with color, imparting a tactile sense of comprehension.
